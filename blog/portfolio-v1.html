Initial commit. Choosing Hugo (the static site generator... or just my name?) but sticking to Vanilla HTML/Tailwind for maximum control.

The Stack

I wanted something fast, bloat-free, and hosting-friendly.

  • HTML5: Semantic and clean.
  • Tailwind CSS: Because writing custom CSS for every margin is 2015.
  • Vanilla JS: No React, no Vue, no hydration errors. Just scripts.

Design Philosophy

Inspired by the "Linear" and "Vercel" aesthetic but with a darker, Cyberpunk twist. I'm aiming for a distinct Developer Experience (DX) feel: Monospace fonts (`JetBrains Mono`), dark greys (`slate-900`), and neon accents.
